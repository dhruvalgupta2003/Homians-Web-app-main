Introduction

What is HOMIANS?

HOMIANS is a healthcare brand that offers customised and Categorised Diets based on your preferences, needs, and health.

Where are the HOMIANS' meals prepared?

HOMIANS meals are created in a variety of Digital Cloud Kitchens based on various categories and with sufficient hygiene and sanitation.

Who prepared the meals of HOMIANS ?

HOMIANS meals are cooked by home chefs, i.e. House Moms, using their cooking expertise and following to our standard operating procedures and guidelines.
